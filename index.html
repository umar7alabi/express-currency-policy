Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of إكسبريس العشاوي. This document outlines how we handle user privacy and data protection. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our app users.

Information Collection and Use

إكسبريس العشاوي stores the names of the currencies that users select for notifications. This information is stored locally on the user's device using SharedPreferences and is not shared with any third parties.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, the app will send notifications based on your selected currency preferences. These preferences are stored locally on your device. No personal data is collected, stored, or used in this process.

Data Retention and Deletion

The app retains your currency preferences as long as the app remains installed on your device. You can delete these preferences at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data from your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

إكسبريس العشاوي is not targeted towards children and does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app complies with all regulations regarding children’s online privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
